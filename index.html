Selecting the Ingredients

We only use high-quality, natural, and eco-friendly ingredients to create our signature formula that makes waxing a breeze for you.

 

Melting and Blending

We melt and blend all of the ingredients together to give our wax a smooth-as-silk texture and dream consistency.

 

Adding Color and Aroma

We add in natural colorants and fragrances to give our wax beads their beautiful colors and aromas.

 

 

Cooling and Solidification

We pour the melted wax into bead-shaped molds after blending it vigorously. This gives them their compact shape that you know and love.

 

 

Quality Control

Last but not least, we regularly sample and test our products for various elements, such as melting point, texture, and adhesion.
